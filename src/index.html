Designed for Maximum Ease of Use

Compact and Convenient

Our cushions fold down into a small size of about 3.5 liters, easily slipping into your backpack. Unfolded, they expand into a full-size cushion, ensuring you have a comfortable seat wherever you land.

Built to Last

Utilizing water-resistant and UV-protected fabric, akin to what's used on boats, each cushion promises durability, easy cleaning, and quick drying. Say goodbye to the dread of getting your gear wet or faded.

Light as a Feather

Our cushions are surprisingly lightweight, starting from a mere 240 grams for the classic version — equivalent to carrying a smartphone. You’ll hardly notice it’s there until you need it.

Accessorize for Ease

Each podpop cushion comes equipped with accessories like 2 carabiners, making it a breeze to carry. We've thought through the little details to enhance your overall experience.
