Who are we

We are a group of dedicated professionals who pursue what we believe in — the transformative power of a qualitative and imaginative work style.

Our inspiration to create podpop stems from a multitude of sources, ranging from airplane seatbelt buckles to the breathtaking hues of a beach sunset. And our products embody the vibrant vibe we’ve strived to capture.

History of creation

Podpop was established based on the strong conviction that ideas have the power to propel us forward. Each place comes with its special charm.

With its captivating maze like canals, Utrecht is a scene straight from a storybook. The tranquil Hague beaches offer a refreshingly calm respite, washing away worries.

Rotterdam, meanwhile, takes our breath away with its soaring skyscrapers, a symbol of human accomplishment. Amsterdam is a bustling hub, always alive with exciting energy.

This variety of environments is where podpop came to life, aiming to find the ideal place to relax, absorb the atmosphere, and spark inspiration.

Our principles at work

The most important thing for people is time. Our company is all about three core principles that drive everything we do.
Сonvenience

First and foremost, convenience is absolutely essential to us. We want our customers to feel like we’ve got their backs every step of the way, from the moment they order to the everyday use of our products.

We get it — life can be hectic, and we’re on a mission to make things as easy and enjoyable as possible.

Improvement

But that’s not all. Improvement is a never-ending journey for us. We thrive on the feedback of our customers because it helps us learn and grow.

We’re constantly searching for valuable feedback to improve our products and services. It’s our goal to reach new heights and provide our customers with the most incredible experience possible.

Individuality

And then there’s the power of individuality. We want to be a brand that stands out from the crowd. We take pride in creating a unique look and feel that leaves a lasting impression.

We want our name to stick with our customers, making us unforgettable. By embracing our own distinct identity, we can boldly shine in a saturated market and form a strong connection with our cherished customers.

So, in a nutshell, our principles of convenience, improvement, and individuality are the driving force behind everything we do.

We’re here to make our customers' experiences seamless, constantly evolving and offer an unforgettable brand that sets us apart from the rest.

Isn’t that something to get excited about? You know it!
